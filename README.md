# Advertising-spend-Vs-Sales-Analysis
📊 Advertising & Sales Analytics Dashboard

🚀 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive and interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze the impact of advertising on sales performance. It provides meaningful insights into business growth by combining marketing data, sales metrics, and regional performance into a single unified view.

The dashboard helps in understanding how effectively advertising investments are converted into revenue, while also identifying trends, high-performing brands, and key markets. It is structured into multiple sections including Overview, Advertising Analysis, Sales Analysis, and Country-Based Analysis, making it easy to explore data from different perspectives.

⸻

🎯 Objectives
	•	Analyze the relationship between advertising spend and sales performance
	•	Evaluate Return on Investment (ROI) for marketing campaigns
	•	Identify top-performing brands and regions
	•	Understand customer demand and order patterns
	•	Optimize budget allocation and business strategy using data insights

⸻

📌 Key Features
	•	📈 Interactive Dashboard with multiple pages and drill-down capabilities
	•	💰 KPI Tracking (Total Sales, Ad Spend, ROI, Cost per Order)
	•	🏷️ Brand-wise Analysis to compare performance and investment
	•	🌍 Country-wise Insights for regional decision-making
	•	📊 Trend Analysis using time-based visualizations
	•	🔍 Correlation Analysis between ad spend and sales using scatter plots
	•	🗺️ Geographical Visualization for global performance insights

⸻

📊 Dashboard Components

🔶 Overview
	•	Displays key metrics like Total Sales (66.72M), Ad Spend (11M), ROI (5.89)
	•	Shows trends of sales vs advertising over time
	•	Highlights brand-wise contribution

🔶 Advertising Analysis
	•	Monthly, brand-wise, and country-wise breakdown of ad spend
	•	Identifies seasonal trends and marketing focus areas
	•	Compares ad spend vs sales performance

🔶 Sales Analysis
	•	Detailed brand-wise and country-wise sales performance
	•	Comparison of Gross Sales, Net Sales, and Total Sales
	•	Identifies top revenue-generating brands

🔶 Country-Based Analysis
	•	Regional insights including sales, ad spend, ROI, and order count
	•	Helps identify high-performing and underperforming markets
	•	Includes map visualization for global analysis

⸻

🛠️ Tech Stack
	•	Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation
	•	DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculations and KPIs
	•	Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
	•	Excel / CSV Dataset – Data source

⸻

📈 Insights Gained
	•	Strong correlation between advertising spend and sales growth
	•	Certain brands generate higher ROI with optimized spending
	•	India and USA are major contributors to revenue
	•	Some regions have higher costs or return rates, indicating improvement areas
